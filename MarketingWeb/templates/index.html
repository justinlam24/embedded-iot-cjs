About SkateTrack

SkateTrack is an advanced skateboard motion tracking system that detects and visualizes your tricks in real-time using a Raspberry Pi with an accelerometer. The system helps skaters analyze their movements and improve performance.

Key Features

Real-Time Motion Tracking

Utilizes a Raspberry Pi with an accelerometer to capture skateboard movements accurately.

3D Trick Visualization

Shows a real-time 3D rendering of your skateboard tricks.

Performance Statistics

Tracks your tricks, speed, and accuracy to help you improve over time.
